# Welcome to bank_categorizer

**Understand your spending without the tedium**

Managing finances shouldn't mean hours spent sorting through transaction lists and manually labeling every purchase. bank_categorizer takes that burden off your shoulders. Whether you're tracking personal expenses, managing a budget, or simply trying to understand where your money actually goes, this tool automates the categorization process so you can focus on what matters—making smarter financial decisions.

## The Problem We Solve

Bank statements are full of transactions, but they don't come pre-organized in a way that makes sense to you. You export a CSV or Excel file from your bank and get hundreds of raw entries with cryptic merchant names and descriptions. Sorting them manually into meaningful categories is error-prone and takes forever. Without organized data, it's hard to answer basic questions like "How much did I spend on groceries this month?" or "Where are my discretionary expenses going?"

## How It Works

bank_categorizer learns your spending patterns using smart keyword matching. Upload your bank statement—in CSV or Excel format—and the system automatically sorts each transaction into the categories that matter to you. It recognizes transactions by description and assigns them intelligently. You get a clear, categorized view of all your spending in seconds. If the system misses something or you want to adjust a categorization, you can manually correct it. No complex setup. No learning curve. Just instant clarity.

## What You Can Do

Once your transactions are organized, powerful insights become simple. Ask real questions and get real answers: What did I spend on dining out? How much went to transportation? What were my total expenses for the month? See spending broken down by category, compare periods to track trends, and identify where your money is going. Use these insights to budget more effectively, spot unnecessary expenses, and make intentional financial choices.

## Get Started

Ready to take control of your spending? Upload your first bank statement and let bank_categorizer do the heavy lifting. Your organized financial picture is just a few clicks away.
